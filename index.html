Who We Are

The UGA Motorcycle Club is a community of passionate riders who share a love for the open road, adventure, and camaraderie. We organize regular rides, events, and gatherings, fostering a strong bond among our members.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a vibrant and inclusive community for motorcycle enthusiasts. We aim to promote safe riding practices, organize exciting group rides, and engage in charitable activities that give back to our community. Whether you're a seasoned rider or just starting, you'll find a welcoming home with us.

  • Regular group rides and events
  • Community service and charity work
  • Skill-building workshops
  • Social gatherings and meetups
